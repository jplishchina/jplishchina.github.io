Our mission is to improve the lives of LGBTQ+ teens.
We aim to do this through education and building a virtual sense of community.
By creating a place for LGBTQ+ teens to connect with each other and themselves,
we believe we can show queer youth how much better a place the world is when you have someone by your side.
By donating, you help us help the community by allowing us to improve and provide more resources to young LGBTQ+ people.
